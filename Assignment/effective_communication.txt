EFFECTIVE COMMUNICATION 

1. how communication is important at work place?
Communication is important at the workplace because it helps everyone understand their tasks and work together better. It prevents misunderstandings, keeps projects running smoothly, and makes it easier to solve problems. Good communication also builds trust and a positive work environment.

2. explain your communication style
My communication style is clear and open. I like to make sure everyone understands the message, so I keep things simple and to the point. I also encourage feedback and listen carefully to others to ensure effective collaboration.


3. importance of communication during interview
Communication is key during an interview because it helps you express your skills and experiences clearly. It also shows how well you listen and respond, which is important to the interviewer. Good communication helps you build a connection and present yourself confidently.
